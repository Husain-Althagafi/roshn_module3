Site Daily Report - MARAFY Waterfront
Date: 21/10/2025
Reporting Officer: Mohammed Al-Harbi
Sector: Marina East

Mixed day with both good progress and setbacks.

Progress achieved:

Marine works: Barge delivered and placed 28 concrete tetrapods for breakwater section BW-3. Marine contractor making good time. Weather window favorable.

Canal lining: Installation of HDPE waterproof liner continued on canal section CS-12. Completed 420 sqm today. Welding quality checked and approved.

Bridge structure: Prestressing cables installed in bridge deck BD-02. All 16 cables tensioned to design spec. Structural engineer signed off.

Paving: Completed asphalt paving of pedestrian pathway along canal from station ST-1 to ST-3. Total 350 meters of pathway now finished. Line marking next.

Incidents and Issues:

SAFETY INCIDENT - MAJOR: At approximately 2:15pm, a scissor lift tipped over in zone M-East while worker was installing signage at 6m height. The worker (Saleh Ahmed, subcontractor) fell and sustained injuries to left arm and ribs. Ambulance called immediately, worker transported to King Fahad Hospital. Incident investigation team formed. All elevated work platforms inspected - found the ground under the failed lift was not properly compacted. Site safety officer has suspended all scissor lift operations until ground conditions verified and documented for all work areas. HSE manager en route to site. Client and PIF safety team notified as per protocol.

Additionally: Shortage of steel reinforcement bars size 20mm. Only 40% of required quantity delivered. Supplier claims production delay at mill. Affects retaining wall work scheduled for next week. Procurement scrambling to find alternate supplier.

Crew count at incident time: 168 workers
Operations continue in unaffected zones.
